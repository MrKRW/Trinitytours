OUR JOURNEY

The Trinity Tours Story

From a small passion project to Sri Lanka's most loved travel company — here are the milestones that shaped who we are.

2010

The Beginning

Trinity Tours was founded by three friends united by their love for Sri Lanka. We started with a single van, one route, and an enormous passion to show travellers the real island beyond the tourist trail.

2013

First International Clients

Word spread fast. Travellers from the UK, Germany, and Australia began booking through us, and we expanded our team of guides to cover the Cultural Triangle and the Hill Country.

2016

Luxury Collection Launch

We launched our premium tier — hand-picked boutique hotels, private chauffeurs, and bespoke itineraries for discerning travellers seeking the finest Sri Lanka has to offer.

2019

Award-Winning Recognition

Trinity Tours received the Sri Lanka Tourism Award for Best Tour Operator, cementing our reputation as the island's most trusted travel partner. We celebrated 25,000 happy travellers.

2022

Digital Transformation

We launched our new digital platform, making it easier than ever to plan, book, and experience Sri Lanka with real-time availability, instant confirmations, and a seamless online journey.

Today

50,000+ Travellers & Growing

With over 50,000 happy travellers, 100+ destinations, and a team of 60+ local experts, Trinity Tours continues to redefine what it means to truly experience Sri Lanka.

OUR SERVICES

What We Offer

TOURS

Explore the little paradise making it a memorable vacation travelling with TT discovering the cultural heritage and natural beauties of the island and the scenic beauty of the hill country not only that the ancient ruins and religious shrines, national parks and wildlife sanctuaries, traditional buildings of the towns and cities and of course the brilliant Sun and Sandy beaches with tailor made tours offered to you with budget friendly packages.

  • Adventure: White water rafting, Snorkeling, hot air balloon ride, Surfing, Jet ski, and many more activities.
  • Beach: Negombo, Mount Lavinia, Bentota, Galle, Unawatuna, Mirissa, Trincomalee, Passikudah and Arugam Bay.
  • Culture & Heritage: Sigiriya, Anuradhapura and Polonnaruwa (cultural triangle).
  • Nature & Wildlife: Elephants to leopards, whales to dolphins and 300-plus endemic species.

ACCOMMODATION

TT offers you a wide range of accommodation from five-star luxury hotels, boutique hotels, resorts, budget hotels to home stay again which fits every budget where anyone can make your desire to happen.

  • Hotels: Experience the Legendary Sri Lankan Hospitality with the Best Price Guaranteed.
  • Boutique Hotels: Cool, calm and collected range of boutique retreats.
  • Villas and Bungalows: Luxury or budgeted villas and bungalows offered all around Sri Lanka.
  • Home stays: For those who want to feel it home and feel the authentic ‘Sri Lankan’ experience.

TRANSPORTATION
